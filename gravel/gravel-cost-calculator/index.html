Reading a gravel quote like an estimator

Three questions that catch every hidden cost

Is the price per ton or per yard (convert before comparing — a $40 yard of crusher run is a $21 ton)? Is delivery included, and at what minimum? And is the material washed/clean or does it carry fines (which changes both the density and the job it can do)? Suppliers answer all three happily — estimators just ask.
