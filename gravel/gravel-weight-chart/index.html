Weight per cubic yard

The cubic yard is the unit most US suppliers load by, and the ton is the unit they weigh by — this table is the bridge between them. All figures are for dry material; a rained-on stockpile can run 10–15% heavier on the scale.

Loose vs compacted

Which figure do you order from?

Order from the loose weight when the supplier sells by loose cubic yard (that's what the truck holds), but size the required volume from the compacted figure whenever the spec calls for a compacted thickness — a 4-inch compacted lift of crusher run needs about 12% more stone than a 4-inch loose one. Getting the two mixed up is the most common reason driveway lifts finish low.
