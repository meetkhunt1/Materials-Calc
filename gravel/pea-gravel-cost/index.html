Bags vs bulk: a 200 ft² patio

At patio scale the answer is not close: 50 ft³ is 100 bags at roughly $500, or about 2.4 tons of bulk stone at $45/ton plus a $75 delivery — around $180 landed. Bulk saves 60% and spares you lifting a hundred 50 lb bags.

The crossover math

Make three phone calls

Call two landscape yards and one quarry before ordering. The quarry gate price is often half the landscape-yard price for identical stone — if you can borrow a trailer and haul a ton yourself, the savings pay for the afternoon.
