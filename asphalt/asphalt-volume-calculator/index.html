Volume-to-tons cheat sheet

At 145 lb/ft³ compacted / 117 loose. Full conversion tables in the cubic yard and cubic foot guides.
VolumeCompacted hot mixLoose hot mix
1 cubic foot145 lb≈117 lb
1 cubic yard1.96 tons≈1.58 tons

Volume is the check, tons are the order

Estimators carry both numbers: volume catches geometric mistakes (does 12 yd³ look right for this driveway?), tonnage matches the plant's scale tickets. When the two disagree with the crew's gut, remeasure before the trucks roll.
